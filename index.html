HOW IT WORKS


The app uses information from the Victoria University Sustainable Organisation. Retrieving information about sustainable news at Victoria University as well as updating frequently with new information, videos and tips. The forum is an interactive tool that students are able to use and interact with other students through. By using this tool, students are able to gain information from students that are in similar living and financial situation to themselves. This information will be stored through the firebase database.
